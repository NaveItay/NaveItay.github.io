Our Specialties

Embedded Engineering

At EATS SYSTEMS, our Embedded Engineering team specializes in developing customized embedded solutions tailored to your specific needs. We leverage the latest microcontrollers and embedded processors to create efficient, reliable systems. Our expertise includes firmware development, real-time operating systems (RTOS), and integration with various sensors and peripherals.

We ensure seamless integration of hardware and software components, resulting in optimized performance and functionality. Our team follows industry best practices to deliver robust and scalable embedded systems that meet the highest quality standards.

Electrical Engineering

Our Electrical Engineering services encompass the design and development of complex electronic circuits and systems. We provide end-to-end solutions from schematic design to prototyping and testing. Our engineers are proficient in analog and digital circuit design, signal processing, and power management.

Utilizing advanced simulation tools and design methodologies, we ensure that our electrical designs are optimized for performance, reliability, and cost-effectiveness. We work closely with clients to meet their specific requirements and compliance standards.

PCB Design

EATS SYSTEMS offers professional PCB Design services for a wide range of applications. Our designers are skilled in creating multilayer PCBs with high-speed signal integrity and efficient power distribution. We handle everything from layout and routing to manufacturing documentation.

We focus on optimizing PCB designs for manufacturability and reliability, considering factors such as thermal management, electromagnetic compatibility (EMC), and mechanical constraints. Our goal is to deliver PCB designs that meet performance objectives while minimizing production costs.

Low Power Systems

Specializing in Low Power Systems, we design solutions that maximize energy efficiency and extend battery life. Our expertise includes selecting low-power components, implementing power-saving modes, and optimizing software algorithms to reduce energy consumption.

Whether you're developing portable devices, IoT sensors, or energy-harvesting systems, we have the knowledge to create power-efficient designs that meet your application's requirements without compromising performance.
